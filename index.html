📍 Area overview

Vega de Villafufre and San Vicente de Toranzo sit in the Valle de Toranzo, roughly 25–35 km south of Santander — prime Territorio Pasiego country.

All rural conversions here fall under LOTUC/Ley 5/2022 and require CROTU authorisation. The Guía de Buenas Prácticas para el Territorio Pasiego may also apply.
